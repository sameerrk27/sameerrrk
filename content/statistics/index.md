# Statistics for Data Analysis

Statistics is the **language of data**. Without it, you can describe what happened but can't say whether it means anything.

## 🗺️ Statistics Roadmap

```
Descriptive Statistics
├── Measures of Center: Mean, Median, Mode
├── Measures of Spread: Variance, Std Dev, IQR, Range
└── Distributions: Normal, Skewed, Bimodal

       ↓

Probability
├── Probability Rules
├── Conditional Probability & Bayes
└── Distributions: Normal, Binomial, Poisson

       ↓

Inferential Statistics
├── Sampling & Estimation
├── Confidence Intervals
└── Hypothesis Testing (t-test, chi-square, ANOVA)

       ↓

Regression Analysis
├── Correlation
├── Simple Linear Regression
└── Multiple Regression
```
